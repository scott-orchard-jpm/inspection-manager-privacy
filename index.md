# Privacy Policy
**Inspection Manager**  
Effective Date: March 4th, 2026

Inspection Manager respects your privacy.

## What Data This App Uses
The app stores information you enter or create, including:
- Inspection details (property code, address, notes, statuses, parts)
- Photos taken for inspections
- App settings (report/logo/email defaults and related preferences)

## Where Data Is Stored
All inspection data and photos are stored locally on your device within the app’s storage.

## Data Collection and Sharing
- We do **not** collect your inspection data on our servers.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.

## When Data Leaves Your Device
Data leaves your device only when you explicitly choose to share it, such as:
- emailing reports from within the app using your device’s mail composer

## Camera and Photo Access
The app requests camera/photo-related permissions only to:
- capture inspection photos
- include photos in reports
- save/access photos needed by app features

## Data Retention and Deletion
Your data remains on your device until you delete it (for example, by deleting inspections, photos, or uninstalling the app).

## Children’s Privacy
This app is not directed to children under 13.

## Contact
If you have privacy questions, contact: inspectionmanager.support@gmail.com
